XPIXEL-SMP: Our Survival World

terrain Custom Terrain Generation

Our world features unique terrain generation that creates stunning landscapes while maintaining the vanilla Minecraft feel. Explore vast mountain ranges, deep canyons, and lush valleys that feel familiar yet fresh.

The world border starts at 10,000 blocks and expands as the community grows, ensuring plenty of space for everyone to build and explore.

store Player-Driven Economy

XPIXEL-SMP features a completely player-run economy with our shopping district at spawn. Players can claim shop plots to sell their goods and services to other players.

We use a diamond-based currency system with custom economy plugins that prevent inflation and keep the market balanced for all players.

group Community Focus

At XPIXEL-SMP, we prioritize building a welcoming community. We have regular community events like build competitions, PvP tournaments, and cooperative projects.

Our staff team is always available to help new players get started and ensure everyone follows our community guidelines.

settings Vanilla+ Experience

We've carefully selected quality-of-life plugins that enhance the vanilla experience without changing the core gameplay. Features include:

  • One-player sleep system
  • Graves to protect items on death
  • Customizable armor stands
  • Player heads as trophies
  • Anti-grief protections

update Regular Updates

Our world is constantly evolving with regular updates that add new content and features based on community feedback. We maintain detailed changelogs and announce all updates in our Discord.

Every 6-12 months, we start a fresh season to give new players a fair start while preserving the old world for nostalgia.

security Server Security

We take server security seriously with advanced anti-cheat systems, regular backups, and a dedicated staff team to monitor and maintain a fair playing environment for all.

Our server features include:

  • 24/7 monitoring
  • Daily backups
  • Anti-xray protections
  • Grief prevention systems
  • Player claim protections

event Special Events

We host regular special events to keep the gameplay fresh and exciting. These include:

  • Seasonal build competitions
  • PvP tournaments
  • Scavenger hunts
  • Community projects
  • Holiday celebrations

Winners receive special rewards and recognition in our community!
