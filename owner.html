About The Owner

Bikram Konai

Owner & Moderator

The creator and main developer of XPIXELSMP. With over 5 years of Minecraft server experience, Bikram has built XPIXELSMP from the ground up to create the perfect survival experience.

"My goal with XPIXELSMP was to create a server where players can enjoy vanilla-like survival with just the right amount of quality-of-life improvements. I wanted a community where everyone can have fun without pay-to-win elements ruining the experience."
