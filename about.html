XPIXEL-SMP: Our Survival World

Custom Terrain Generation

Our world features unique terrain generation that creates stunning landscapes while maintaining the vanilla Minecraft feel. Explore vast mountain ranges, deep canyons, and lush valleys that feel familiar yet fresh.
World border starts at 10,000 blocks, expanding as the community grows.

Player-Driven Economy

Our shopping district at spawn allows players to claim shop plots, selling goods and services using a diamond-based currency and custom economy plugins.

Community Focus

Regular events, build competitions, PvP tournaments, and cooperative projects build a welcoming, fun community. Staff always available!

Vanilla+ Experience

  • One-player sleep system
  • Graves to protect items on death
  • Customizable armor stands
  • Player heads as trophies
  • Anti-grief protections

Regular Updates

We add new content based on feedback, announce updates in Discord, and start fresh seasons every 6-12 months for fairness!

Server Security

  • 24/7 monitoring
  • Daily backups
  • Anti-xray protections
  • Grief prevention systems
  • Player claim protections

Special Events

Seasonal build competitions, PvP tournaments, scavenger hunts, community projects, and holiday celebrations with special rewards!
